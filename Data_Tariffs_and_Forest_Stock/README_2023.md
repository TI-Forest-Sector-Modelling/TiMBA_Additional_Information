## README

This dataset serves as a guide for understanding how the ad valorem
tariffs used in TiMBA - a partial economic equilibrium model for the
global forest products market were generated and updated based on the
WTO\'s tariff data and. Further, this dataset contains information on
forest growing stock data and forest stock growth rates as well as on
forest area data and forest area growth rates for 180 countries, as
considered in the TiMBA model. The data are essential for modelling
scenarios on forest development and trade flows. By following the
explanations outlined in the respective Manual
(Scenario_input_data_generation_TradeTariffs_Forest_2025), we ensure
transparency in mean ad valorem tariff calculations as well as forest
growing stock data and annual changes across the countries listed. For
any further details, the tariff and forest data can be accessed directly
through the provided database sources. Processed data related to forests
are provided in the csv files.
